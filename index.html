Luca's Story

I've lived in this city for three years. Most of my time was spent rushing to classes, working part-time, and just trying to survive. I rarely stopped to really look at it. The supermarket I usually went to is just around the corner; after 10 PM, it was so empty, feeling like a moment that belonged only to me. That desk lamp by the window accompanied me through many late nights and witnessed the moment I decided to leave. I didn't leave anything important behind, but if you see these, perhaps for a split second, you'll feel that someone once lived in this city just like this.
